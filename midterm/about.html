Why have profile pictures?

and why do they matter so much?

That first look comes from their profile picture. It gives a quick clue about the person. It shapes our first impression. And first impressions stick. They help us guess what someone likes or how they act. Maybe it's their pet, which can tell you if they're a cat or dog person. Maybe it's a picture of a video game or a book character they enjoy. Some people pick the plain default picture, a gray silhouette. It might mean they keep things private or just don't bother. Either way, a profile picture can be the first way you present yourself online. Everyone sees it in chats or shares. It says more about you without a word.

So why backgrounds/wallpapers?

Wallpapers can be used for visual appeal and to personalize a user's device to reflect the user's personality, interests, hobbies, aspirations, or simply what they think is a nice picture and fits their vibe. They make your screen feel like yours. A special thank you to pinterest for providing the images.
